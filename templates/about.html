Welcome to OurCalendar

Have you ever hosted an event or meeting with a handful of participants where
finding the right time that fit everyone's schedule, took longer than the time
needed to conduct the meeting. Even with tools like google calendar and
calendaly, this problem is not solved.

OurCalendar is an app enabled to make scheduling automatic.

Now with OurCalendar,
any user who has an account can simply invite users to an event, setting some
preferences for time of day and duration, and OurCalendar will automate finding
a day and time that suits EVERYONE's schedule and send out all calendar invites.
